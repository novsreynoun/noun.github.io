What fans call "managers" are usually what GMMTV calls "ARs".An AR is a GMMTV representative assigned to take care of an artist. Their main function seems to be making sure artists go to events and filming sets as smoothly as planned and assisting them in various activities related to works. They also represent the artists when someone initially try to contact or hire them (they would be the first person you talk on the phone when you want to hire an artist to your event, etc.), and sometimes act as the intermediary between the artists and fans or other parties as well.
